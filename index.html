About

A trusted partner at the decision point.

Glenloch Advisory is an independent practice offering senior, hands-on counsel to leaders facing pivotal moments — a transaction, a restructuring, a strategic shift, or a question without an obvious answer.

We work quietly and selectively, alongside a small number of clients at a time. The engagement is direct: no layers, no handoffs, and no agenda beyond your interests. What we bring is judgement earned across boardrooms and balance sheets, and the candour to tell you what we actually think.
